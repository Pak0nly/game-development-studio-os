## Evidence Rules

- **Assessment:** Crime99 relevance, adaptation effort, overlap, and risk are project-specific judgements, not publisher claims.
- **Preflight-only:** mesh contents, exact counts, scale, pivots, colliders, LOD quality, shader behaviour, material conversion, camera readability, performance, and Unity 6.3 behaviour require a separately authorised download/import preflight.
- A store row such as `2021.3 Compatible` is not evidence of Unity 6.3 compatibility. Only an explicit Unity 6000 row or official publisher statement is treated as Unity 6 evidence.
- Prices are the signed-in EUR prices shown on 2026-07-14, exclusive of checkout tax where the store states this. `Not shown` means the current product page no longer exposed a purchasable listing.

## Duplicate and Overlap Findings

- `Fuyoh Cyberpunk Diner` and `Yellow Parrot Restaurant` overlap strongly; preflight should select the better exterior/prop source rather than import both by default.
- `1950s NYC`, `Cyberpunk Port City`, `Victorian Market`, `Mid-Century Data Centre`, `Military Base`, and `Futuristic Rooftop City` all contribute urban/industrial structures. A bounded selection is essential to avoid multi-gigabyte duplication and style fragmentation.
- `Medieval Village`, `Roman Street`, `Bandit Valley`, and `Witch Village` overlap as street/village construction sources but are weak thematic fits.
- `Simple Bundle` overlaps nearly every visual category and should not be imported wholesale.
- `POLYGON Particle FX Pack` likely makes a new general-purpose stylised fire/explosion pack redundant until preflight disproves coverage.
