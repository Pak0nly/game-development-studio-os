## Purpose

This file stores valuable ideas that are not part of the current committed scope.

Parking Lot content is:

- not approved;
- not scheduled;
- not authoritative;
- not part of the first prototype.

Ideas may later move into another GDD module after explicit review.

## Usage Rules

Each idea should include:

- short description;
- potential value;
- major cost or risk;
- review trigger.

Do not use Parking Lot ideas as implementation requirements.

## Game Mode Ideas

## Zombie Outbreak

A spreading city outbreak using civilians, traffic, vehicles, and destruction.

Potential value:

- strong cooperative mode;
- reuses city systems;
- creates crowd pressure;
- supports vehicle rescue and crowd control.

Risks:

- new infection AI;
- large crowd cost;
- genre saturation;
- major balance work.

Review after:

- combat bots and city population succeed.

## Golden Car

A valuable vehicle becomes the moving objective.

Potential value:

- vehicle-focused competition;
- constant movement;
- clear objective;
- bot compatibility.

Risks:

- one dominant strategy;
- vehicle destruction rules;
- spawn camping.

Review after:

- networked FFA and TDM.

## Most Value Collected

Players collect or bank the most value.

Potential value:

- greed versus safety;
- strong map movement;
- supports theft and interception.

Risks:

- requires economy rules;
- possible passive play;
- unclear banking flow.

Review after:

- economy direction is validated.

## King of the Road

Players score by controlling a vehicle, route, or road objective.

Potential value:

- emphasises driving;
- creates moving conflict;
- supports teams.

Risks:

- route repetition;
- map dependency;
- vehicle dominance.

Review after:

- vehicle and multiplayer foundations succeed.

## Review Process

An idea leaves the Parking Lot only when:

1. a concrete player problem or opportunity exists;
2. its pillar support is clear;
3. prototype or production timing is appropriate;
4. major risks are understood;
5. it receives explicit approval;
6. the relevant GDD module is updated.

## Authorisation Boundary

Parking Lot content does not authorise any implementation, purchase, research, or production work.
