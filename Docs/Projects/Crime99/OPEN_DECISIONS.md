## Purpose and Policy

This is the central register for unresolved and recently resolved Crime99 project decisions. It uses four states only:

* `Active` — a Human Developer decision or later authorised analysis is required;
* `Deferred` — deliberately postponed and outside current prototype scope;
* `Needs Prototype Validation` — evidence from separately authorised prototype work is required;
* `Closed` — the current answer is confirmed and retained as decision evidence.

Closed Studio OS decisions are referenced, not reopened. Nothing in this register authorises architecture, implementation, purchase, subscription, paid external calls, download, installation, repository creation, staging, commit, or push.

[`PROTOTYPE_ROADMAP.md`](PROTOTYPE_ROADMAP.md) is the canonical source for the Asset Pre-Production and Asset and Style Preflight sequence as well as the prototype gate order. This register records decision state and dependencies without duplicating that sequence.
